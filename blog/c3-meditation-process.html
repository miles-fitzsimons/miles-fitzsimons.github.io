Meditation Process

21 April 2016

How did the 'process over product' concept affect the way you tackled the site redesign and rebuild?

The process over product concept allowed me to step back and simplify my site redesign with no regrets. In fact, I probably gained a deeper understanding of the fundamentals of framework design by focusing on a simple design that works how I want it to, rather than focusing on a complicated design that probably wouldn't have worked well.

What did you think about mediation before reading chapter 2 of 'Search Inside Yourself'?

My perception of meditation is that it is mental brain training with the goal of achieving calm, clarity, happiness and peace.

I have never meditated but a lot of my friends do and I have been thinking about giving it a go.

What new things have you learnt about mediation?

Meditation is similar to exercise in many regards. Like pumping weights at the gym will grow your muscles, meditation will exercise your brain. The more you do it, the better you will become and the easier it will be to achieve calm and happiness, not only when meditating but at all times.

Did any of the suggested mediation techniques stand out to you?

I liked the concept of starting with an intention, even if it is as simple as creating good intentions. I also liked the concept of becoming aware of your attitude towards yourself. I think a lot of people can be overly hard on themselves in an often unjustified manner. I am guilty of this behaviour from time to time. Finally, I liked the concept of experiencing something without judging or reacting. This is a hard thing for me to do.
